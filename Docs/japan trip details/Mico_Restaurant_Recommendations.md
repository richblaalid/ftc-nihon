## Osaka

| Restaurant | Specialty | Location | Notes |
|------------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Hanamaruken** | Slow-braised pork rib ramen | Dotonbori | Rich broth, big portions, free soup refills |
| **Kushikatsu Daruma** ⭐ | Original kushikatsu (fried skewers) | Shinsekai | No double dipping! Iconic, old-school |
| **Kogaryu Takoyaki** | Soft, flavorful takoyaki | Amemura (near Triangle Park) | Try black pepper or green onion ponzu |
| **Hananoki** | Okonomiyaki | Kuromon Market area | Mico's personal favorite — family-run, generations old |
| **Uomarushoten** | Fresh sashimi | Kuromon Market area | Morning auction fish, incredible value for chutoro/otoro |
| **Niku no Tenmaya Kaguratei** | High-quality wagyu | Osaka | Try thick-cut wagyu tongue |
| **魚屋のトラットリア アレグロペッシェ** | Japanese-Italian seafood | Near Namba | Fresh fish market sourcing, try seafood carpaccio |
| **Stand Afro** | Standing bar with karaage | Osaka | Great for meeting locals |
| **So-Ken** | Sake bar (30+ varieties) | Osaka | Self-service style, rare sake finds |
| **L&L Bar & Restaurant** | Mediterranean/European | Amemura | Try the flaming cocktail |
| **Bar Amaro** | Signature cocktails | Shinsaibashi | Intimate 12-seat gem |
