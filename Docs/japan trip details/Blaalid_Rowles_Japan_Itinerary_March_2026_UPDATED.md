## Etiquette Guide for the Family

### Train & Station Behavior
| ‚úÖ Do | ‚ùå Don't |
|------|---------|
| Walk to side to check maps | Stop suddenly in walkways |
| Use quiet voices | Use phone speaker or FaceTime |
| Wear backpacks on chest when crowded | Block aisles or doors |
| Give up Priority Seats for elderly/disabled | Assume seats are yours |
| Queue in marked lines on platform | Push or cut in line |

### Restaurant Etiquette
| ‚úÖ Do | ‚ùå Don't |
|------|---------|
| Use payment tray for cash/cards | Hand money directly |
| Say "Itadakimasu" before eating | Start eating immediately |
| Keep orders simple | Heavily customize orders |
| Accept gracefully if turned away (space) | Take rejection personally |
| Finish everything on your plate | Leave lots of food |

### Walking & Public Spaces
| ‚úÖ Do | ‚ùå Don't |
|------|---------|
| Step aside to finish food | Eat while walking |
| Carry small trash bag (bins rare!) | Drop trash or leave it |
| Use umbrella stands/bags at shops | Drip water inside stores |
| Keep umbrella tip low when closed | Swing at child height |
| Stand on correct escalator side | Block both sides |

### Ryokan & Tatami Rules
| ‚úÖ Do | ‚ùå Don't |
|------|---------|
| Remove ALL shoes at genkan (entry) | Walk on tatami with any footwear |
| Wear provided slippers in hallways | Wear slippers on tatami |
| Socks or bare feet only on tatami | Step on edge of tatami mats |

### Onsen (Hot Spring) Etiquette
| ‚úÖ Do | ‚ùå Don't |
|------|---------|
| Wash/rinse thoroughly BEFORE entering bath | Skip the wash station |
| Keep voices low | Be loud or splashy |
| Place small towel on head (not in water) | Put towel in bath water |
| Enter slowly, relax | Jump or dive in |
| Use private/family bath if kids are rowdy | Disturb other bathers |
